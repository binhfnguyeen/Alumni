One of the main attractions of Vũng Tàu is its picturesque coastline, with long stretches of sandy beaches bathed in warm tropical sunlight. Visitors can bask in the sun, take a refreshing dip in the clear blue waters, or engage in various water sports such as swimming, surfing, and jet skiing. The most well-known beaches in Vũng Tàu include Back Beach (Bãi Trước) and Front Beach (Bãi Sau), each offering its own unique charm and ambiance.
